Product & business

  • Decentralized social media Launched in 2018 and owned by
    TiENCHAT a Singapore corporation, TiENCHAT supports the basics
    like voice chatting, picture messaging, and video calling. Users
    can also share your real-time location with friends, play
    mini-games with each other, and post to a Story-like feature
    called "moments." This app comes with a localization feature
    that will translate messages and content between 4 different
    languages. However, it's not nearly as secure as other messaging
    apps like Signal or Telegram. The app comes with a P2P
    encryption. TiENCHAT is free to download for iPhone and Android users.
